Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

How long do I have access?
Lifetime. Once you purchase, the training and all bonuses are yours forever, including any updates we add.
How much should I budget for ad spend?
Start with $5/day (about $150/month). That’s enough to test, learn, and see real results. Scale up when you’re ready.
Do I need Zapier?
If you use the native Facebook form (our recommendation), yes. The starter plan is $29.99/month. We walk you through setup. Even with that cost, native forms are significantly cheaper per lead than landing pages.
I already have The Wellness Pro Website System. How does this fit?
WPWS builds your website. Ads Made Simple drives the right people to it. Your website is the engine. Ads are the fuel. They work together beautifully.
I’ve never run an ad before. Can I still do this?
This was built for you. We start from zero. By the end, you’ll have a live campaign running. No prior experience needed.
Does this work for Instagram too?
Yes! Facebook and Instagram ads run through the same platform (Meta Ads Manager). Your ads can show on both automatically.
What if something goes wrong?
The Troubleshooting module covers the most common issues: ad rejections, weird results, account flags. You’ll know what to do.

We cannot guarantee results. Ad performance depends on your offer, your audience, your ad spend, and a lot of variables that are outside our control. What we can guarantee is that the training is thorough, the support is real, and everything we teach is what we actually use. Because of the instant access nature of this program, all sales are final.
