Questions? We’ve Got Answers.

How much should I budget for ad spend?
Start with $5/day (about $150/month). That’s enough to test, learn, and see real results. Scale up when you’re ready.
Do I need Zapier?
If you use the native Facebook form (our recommendation), yes — you’ll need your own Zapier account (the starter plan is $29.99/month), but we set it all up for you. Even with that cost, native forms are significantly cheaper per lead than landing pages.
I already have The Wellness Pro Website System. How does this fit?
WPWS builds your website. Ads Made Simple drives the right people to it. Your website is the engine. Ads are the fuel. They work together beautifully.
I’ve never run an ad before. Is that a problem?
Not at all — you don’t run anything. We do. We build and launch your campaign for you, starting from zero. No prior experience needed, because the technical part is never on your plate.
Does this work for Instagram too?
Yes! Facebook and Instagram ads run through the same platform (Meta Ads Manager). Your ads can show on both automatically.
How long does it take from purchase to launch?
About 2–3 weeks in most cases. Once you enroll, you’ll go through onboarding, where we collect the information we need from you and get access to your accounts (or help you set them up if any are missing). From there, we build and launch your first campaign within 2–3 weeks. Every timeline is a little different depending on what accounts need to be set up, your response times, and any edits you’d like along the way.
What happens after the 1-week oversight period?
Your campaign keeps running on autopilot, and your reference library is yours to keep. Inside, you’ll find plain-language walkthroughs of everything we built — how to read the numbers in your ad account so you know what’s working and what to adjust, how a campaign gets built end-to-end (so you can spin up new ones yourself later), and a full troubleshooting section for whenever you need it. We’re also always just a message away if you want to bring us back in for a new campaign or another set of eyes.
What’s your refund policy? Do you guarantee results?
We cannot guarantee results. Ad performance depends on your offer, your audience, your ad spend, and a lot of variables that are outside our control. What we can guarantee is that we build your system with the same strategy we use in our own campaigns, the support is real, and everything is done for you exactly as promised. Because we begin your build immediately after purchase, all sales are final. We encourage you to read through the page carefully and reach out with any questions before purchasing (send questions to info@whitneybateson.com).
